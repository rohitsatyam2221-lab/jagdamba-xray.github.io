Diagnostic Services

What we offer

From chest imaging to cardiac monitoring — complete diagnostic services under one roof.

Digital Chest X-Ray
High-definition chest imaging for lungs, heart size, ribs, and thoracic structures. Used for TB screening, pneumonia, and pre-operative evaluation.
  • Lungs & bronchial assessment
  • Cardiomegaly detection
  • TB & pneumonia screening
  • Instant digital output
Digital ECG
12-lead electrocardiogram to evaluate heart rhythm, detect arrhythmias, and assess cardiac health. Essential before any surgery or for chest pain evaluation.
  • 12-lead computerized ECG
  • Arrhythmia & rhythm analysis
  • Pre-operative cardiac clearance
  • Printed report with interpretation
Bone & Joint X-Ray
Detailed skeletal imaging for fractures, arthritis, and orthopedic conditions. Spine, extremity, and joint views available.
  • Fracture detection & staging
  • Spine & vertebral imaging
  • Knee, hip, shoulder joints
  • Hands, feet, and extremities
